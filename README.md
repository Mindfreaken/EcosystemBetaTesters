# Project EcoSystem

## What is Project EcoSystem?

Project EcoSystem was born out of the direct frustrations I experienced as an esports organization owner. The current landscape is fragmented, requiring teams to use dozens of different platforms for everything from recruiting and contract management to scheduling scrims and hosting tournaments. This leads to a lack of transparency and significant inefficiency.

Project EcoSystem aims to solve these problems by creating a **unified social media and management platform** for esports..and more later.

---

## Key Problems We Are Solving:

* **Fragmented Operations:** No more jumping between 10 different sites and 25 Discord servers for tournaments, scrims, and recruiting. Project EcoSystem aims unifies these functions in one place.
* **Inefficient Management:** The platform will provide better tools for recruiting talent, managing contracts, and handling volunteers.
* **Lack of Transparency:** Project EcoSystem is built to foster open communication and prevent the "shady" behavior that can plague the industry.

## Our Vision

We call it the "Ecosystem" because it's more than just a single tool—it's a connected network designed to help the esports industry evolve. Our mission is to fundamentally change how social media and esports platforms are built and managed. We will directly tackle the industry's greed and lack of transparency by:

* **Operating with Radical Transparency:** We plan to be as close to open source as possible. This includes being transparent about our code, our finances (costs and profits), and our management decisions.
* **Embracing Community-Led Governance:** We will find a tactful way to ensure community members have a single, meaningful vote in key decisions. We want to be held accountable to the community we serve, not to private investors.
* **Rejecting "Dirty Money":** Our funding model will rely solely on community support and profits from the platform itself, not on private equity investments.

By putting the community first, we aim to create a more streamlined, transparent, and professional environment for everyone involved, proving that a different kind of platform is not only possible but necessary.
